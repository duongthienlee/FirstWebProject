Helsinki City Marathon

Helsinki City Marathon is the second largest marathon event in Scandinavia, according to the official race website (www.helsinkicitymarathon.fi).

" The event Sponsored by Gatorade." "The 29th Helsinki City Marathon 2009 was held on 15 August on a route through the city of Helsinki."

"The marathon route in Helsinki is one of the most beautiful worldwide. Almost half of the route runs along the Baltic Sea coastline. The magnificent parks in Helsinki make the running experience unforgettable."

"Here is statistical information about the tournament: In 2011 there were 5430 runners from 47 countries. In 2010, 6,467 runners from 56 countries was in the start line. In 2009, 6,041 runners registered for the marathon from 46 different countries. In 2008, there were over 6,400 runners from 46 different countries. In 2006, there were 6,300 runners from 35 countries who participated."
